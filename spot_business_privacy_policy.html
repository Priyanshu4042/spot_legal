Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 August 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Spot Business mobile application (“the App”). This policy applies only to the Spot Business app and not to the consumer-facing Spot app.

Who We Are

The Spot Business app is operated by the owner of the Spot platform. Contact email: wizverx.spot@gmail.com
Contact phone: +91 95653 79440
The Spot platform is currently not a registered business entity.

Purpose of the App

Spot Business is used by restaurants, eateries, and event organizers to manage their listings on the Spot app, receive and process order details, and manage reservation requests.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and phone number of the business account holder.
  • Orders & Reservations: Order details and reservation requests from customers via the Spot app.
  • Payment History: Records of payouts made to the business.
  • Menu Listings: Businesses can upload their menu items to be displayed in the Spot app.

We do not collect sensitive personal data or use analytics in the Spot Business app.

How We Use the Information

  • To authenticate your account and manage business access to the Spot platform.
  • To deliver notifications related to orders, reservations, and payouts.
  • To process and record payouts to the business.

Third-Party Services

  • MSG91: Used to send OTPs to the registered phone number for authentication purposes.

Data Retention

Transactional records (orders, reservations, payouts) are retained indefinitely at this time. Business accounts are deleted immediately upon request.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared with service providers (e.g., MSG91) only as necessary to operate the App.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including secure authentication and controlled access. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your business account at any time by contacting us at wizverx.spot@gmail.com. Deletion is effective immediately and permanently removes your account.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the Spot Business app and will take effect immediately upon posting.
